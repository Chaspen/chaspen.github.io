Mastodon Vs Twitter
The rising popularity of Social Media is a blessing and a curse. On one hand,
you can become friends with people who live in entirely different countries.
(An example of this would be a few of mine being from Canada, Belgium, Egypt
and Brazil respectivley). But on another hand these companies can be money hungry,
scummy mega-corporations. An example of this would be Facebook actively tracking
users almost everywhere they go, among other things. Here is just a short list of the
scummy or questionable things Facebook has done:
  • Facebook bans 'offensive' Swedish breast cancer awareness video
  • Facebook Will Force Advertising on Ad-Blocking Users
  • Facebook is using your phone's location to suggest new friends
  • Facebook’s ad platform now guesses at your race based on your behavior
  • Facebook Will Start Tracking Which Stores You Walk Into
  • Facebook’s Five New Reaction Buttons: Data, Data, Data, Data, and Data
  • Publisher's Facebook page deleted after posting criticism of Turkish government
  • Lawsuit claims Facebook illegally scanned private messages
    • So it's obvious Facebook isn't exactly the most morally correct corporation,
    what other options would we have then? Sadly, only about 3 vaible ones:
     Instagram, Twitter, and Mastodon. There's some problems with censorship
    and hivemind mentality with Twitter and Instagram but litteraly anything is
    better then the dumpster fire that is Facebook.
    Twitter
    Twitter's main focus is "news" and short 140 character posts, the main pro's
    here is that it's designed so that you can read alot in a short amount of time.
    (Which is admittedly what I use it for), and it's a great platform for shitposting.
    (Which is also admittedly what I use it for) and You can find tons of talented
    artists and musicians on the site. And that's about where the pro's stop. Twitter
    has problems with free-speech. Twitter seemingly can't differentiate between
    something meant to harm, and a joke. An example of this would be that last year,
    I Hate Everything was suspended for posting a "violent threat"     and that his account
    "will not be restored" the culprit being a tweet saying "I'm going to kill your entire
    family". Sounds concerning at first, until you find out the person he sent this to is
    his brother.

    Twitter also seems to be completley blind when it comes to pedophiles on their
    service. There's an alarming amount of actual child porn and child molesters that
    just hang around on Twitter for weeks or months but Twitter is quick to ban/suspend
    you if you jokingingly threaten a friend or family member. Twitter focuses more on
    "Decreasing harassment" and making their site a safe-space rather then focusing on
    more serious things such as this.

    Much like other social media platforms, Twitter doesnt prioritize your privacy. I will give
    credit where it's due though, Twitter's tracking and data collecting is nowhere near as bad
    as Facebook and Instagram. But still, users should have every right to protect their personal
    information from corporate hands.
    Mastodon
    Mastodon has mostly the same premise as Twitter, designed in a way so that you can read
    as much as possible in a short amount of time. The only differences is that Mastodon is split
    into "Instances" Which are almost like entireley different social media sites, but they're still
    under Mastodon in a way. It's hard to explain. But one example would be me having an account
    on Mastodon's own official instance: mastodon.social, and another on chitter.xyz, a mastodon
    instance aimed at techfurs. Mastodon is built this way so that it will never truly be dead. As every
    instance is hosted on its own server. This can also somewhat circumvent government censorship
    and ISP throttling.

    It depends on the instance but i'd say around %99 of mastodon instances respect you as a user, and
    your privacy. This is mostly because alot of Mastodon users share the same mindset. You can even
    add features to your instance, or improve it from the regular mastodon.social instance, such as
    increasing the character limit for Toot's, or having a random picture display in the corner of the
    screen. Or you can even change it entireley and customize it your liking.
