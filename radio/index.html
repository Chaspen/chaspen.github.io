one of the videos, they linked an online shortwave radio tuner. Naturally I checked it out, but I ended up
finding much stranger things(Atleast in my opinion.) Here is a collection of some of the weird shit i've found.
This is what sounds like someone speaking saying English sentences, then repeating
it in Mandarin(Or the other way around, who knows?). It gets weirdly religous near the end for some reason, too.
This sounds like two people speaking either Serbian or Russian, or some other slavic language.
I don't know what the hell this is, sounds like some sort of data is being sent to someone.
